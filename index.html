About Me

I'm a first-year Mechanical Engineering student at Ohio Northern University with a strong foundation in CAD, additive manufacturing, and hands-on fabrication. My engineering interests span product design, manufacturing processes, and robotics — with a particular appreciation for making things that work in the real world.

Before college, I ran a freelance 3D modeling and printing business for three years, delivering custom parts and functional prototypes to local clients. I've since expanded into CNC laser cutting, press brake operation, and programming work at an industrial manufacturer, while contributing to ONU's SAE Baja team.

I bring the same attention to detail whether I'm designing a print-in-place mechanism in Fusion 360 or writing a MATLAB pathfinding simulation — I care about getting it right.
